About Me

Shoshana Klein is a Senior Majoring in Graphic Design at Binghamton University. She is completing her BA this Spring and will be graduating with a Masters in Business Administration Spring 2023. Along with graphic design, she is a semi-professional photographer running her own photography business.

Shoshana is from Bergenfield, New Jersey and has lived in the same house her entire life. She is passionate about her work and looks to excel in everything that she does.
